Our Story

PitchFrame was born from a simple observation: brilliant startup ideas were failing not because they lacked merit, but because they lacked the right guidance and feedback.

Founded in 2023 by a team of entrepreneurs, investors, and AI researchers, we recognized that the traditional startup ecosystem was leaving many promising ventures behind. Access to quality mentorship was limited, feedback was inconsistent, and the process of refining pitches was often inefficient.

We set out to change this by combining the power of artificial intelligence with the wisdom of experienced mentors, creating a platform that could provide instant, actionable feedback while connecting startups with the right experts.

Our Journey

From concept to platform, here's how PitchFrame evolved

Q1 2023

The Idea

Recognizing the gap in startup mentorship, our founders began developing the concept for an AI-powered pitch analysis platform.

Q2 2023

Development Begins

Assembled a team of AI researchers, developers, and startup veterans to build the core platform and AI analysis engine.

Q3 2023

Beta Launch

Launched our beta platform with 50 selected startups and 20 expert mentors, gathering valuable feedback and refining our approach.

Q4 2023

Public Launch

Officially launched PitchFrame to the public, opening our platform to startups worldwide and expanding our mentor network.
